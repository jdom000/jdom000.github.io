Bootstrap has its own responsive paddings and margins Wherein its classes m stands for margins p stands for paddings
Bootstrap padding and margins can be applied on specific sides
  • t-sets either margin or padding at the top of our element: mt or pt
  • b-set either margin or padding at the bottom side of our element: mb or pb
  • y- sets either margin or padding on y-axis, on both top and bottom: my or py
  • x- sets either margin or padding on x-axis, both left and right sides: mx or px
  • l-sets either margin or padding on the left side : ml or pl
  • r- sets either margin or padding on the right side: mr o pr
  • blank- sets margin on all sides
